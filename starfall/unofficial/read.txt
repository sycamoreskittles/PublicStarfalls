This is the folder for unofficial starfall submissions, likely from a pull request.
The conduct goes:
  1. Do not submit malicious starfalls. Chips meant to target, hinder, or disturb players/servers are forbidden.
  2. If someone other than yourself helped you code, please credit them. Specific parts being credited is not required, however include them in the authors parameter.
  3. Use of any of these starfalls is at your own risk. Misuse is not on the author, or on me. It is on the user.
  4. Please try to optimize your chips. This does not require it to be using less than 100 CPUs, but it should not be averaging 500+ CPUs.
  5. If possible, make it an @shared starfall, or @client to conserve server resources, especially if they use a large CPU amount.
  6. You agree that when submitting a starfall, your code may be modified and used by other users.
